Research

My research objective is to develop general-purpose embodied agents that can reason, plan, and act in open-world environments, much like humans. Motivated by this vision, I explore how to scale sensorimotor learning through sim-to-real, build robotic foundation models on heterogeneous data sources, and integrate reactive control with semantic reasoning to solve complex, long-horizon tasks in the physical world.

Key areas of focus include:

  • machine learning for decision making and control
  • robot learning systems that scale with broad sources of data
  • data engines for embodied AI and robotics research
