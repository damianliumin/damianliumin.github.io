Research

My research interest lies in the intersection of machine learning and robotics. The ultimate goal of my research is to build general-purpose robotic systems that perform a large variety of tasks in unstructured environments, thereby assisting people in their daily activities.

To achieve the goal, my primary focus is on the following topics:

  • Simulation environments that facilitate the development and evaluation of new algorithms
  • Acquisition of motor skills in reliable, efficient, scalable and self-supervised manner
  • Embodied agents that can interact with humans, generalize to diverse environments, and plan over long-horizon tasks

During my undergraduate studies, I also worked on NLP and trustworthy ML.
