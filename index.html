Research

I am interested in developing general-purpose embodied agents that can learn diverse skills from extensive offline datasets and continually improve their capabilities through online interactions.
Key areas of focus include:

  • machine learning algorithms deployable in physical world
  • robot learning systems that scale with broad sources of data
  • data engines for embodied AI and robotics research
